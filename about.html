About Us

Innovation that excites. Excellence that inspires.

Our Vision

To provide a strong Christian foundation for the moral and educational development of every child who has the privilege of coming into any of our schools. We build the best in partnership with parents.

Our Mission

To mould lives, give every child equal opportunity to acquire quality education, and instill confidence. We aim to reshape society by empowering every child's conscience and intelligence.

Our Philosophy

Education without discrimination by religion, politics, tribe, race, class, or sex. Admission and promotion are purely on academic merit, encouraging students to be their best.

Welcome

A Tradition of Excellence

Gladys School is a private yet affordable school offering a stimulating curriculum and a supportive environment to ensure our students experience success every day. We focus on scholastic development, personal growth, and ethical enrichment. The result: students who are independent and confident, inquisitive and enthusiastic, responsible, and compassionate.

We believe the school years are the most formative of a child’s life. These early years can breed intellectual apathy or curiosity to seek knowledge. It is a time marked by creative, social, and emotional development, and characterized by a lifelong love of learning and the joys of new, diverse friendships.

Our Legacy

A Place to Be

There are schools and there are schools, but Gladys stands tall amongst its peers for cogent reasons that cannot be overlooked. Founded in September 1999 with academic activities kicking off in October of the same year, the sky since then has been the beginning of our aspirations.

Our School provides students with excellent academic programs and a nurturing environment with close student-teacher relationships. We prepare students for success both personally and academically.

Beyond Academics

Core Values & Activities

This is what Gladys school stands for and we are not ready to bend it for anything.

Celebrating Moulded Lives

Celebrating moulded lives is part of our call. Graduating students are major in this area. After a worthwhile college experience, it is only natural to be celebrated out after a good refining and shaping process.

Inter-House Sports

All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. At Gladys, we host an Inter-house Sports Competition. The school is mindful of this, and over the years, it has always been fun-filled.

Extracurricular Excursions

We have a strong bias for co-curricular activities. We take our students to yearly excursions to places of excitement and learning, like Olumo Rock, Mr. Fish Water Zoo, and the Stock Exchange.

Visit to Orphanages

We leave no stone unturned to give learners an all-round education. A visit to the orphanage with gifts is an avenue we explore to sharpen the lives of learners in the aspect of giving.
