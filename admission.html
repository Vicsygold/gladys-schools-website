How to Apply

Admissions Process

Admission is in progress into all classes for the academic session. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds to experience our rich curriculum and supportive environment.

1

Obtain Form & Prospectus

Forms and Prospectus are available for sale in the school between 8:00 am – 4:00 pm (Mon-Fri).

2

Entrance Examination

Entrance examinations are conducted yearly. Each candidate is tested in Mathematics, English Language, and General Paper.

3

Interview

Successful candidates from the entrance examination are interviewed along with their parents before final admission is offered.

Academics

Academic Standing

The school curriculum is very broad and quite stimulating. We operate the Federal Ministry of Education policy with the early years leading to Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) as well as the Senior Secondary Education.

We are proud to offer a comprehensive range of subjects, catering to diverse career paths and interests. All subjects in the following departments are offered:

  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Commercial Subjects
  • Arts & Humanities
